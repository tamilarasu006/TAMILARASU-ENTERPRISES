Who We Are

India's Trusted Fresh Produce Exporter

TAMILARASU ENTERPRISES is a fresh produce import and export company based in Uthiramerur, Tamil Nadu, India. We source premium fruits, vegetables, grains, and spices directly from Indian farms and deliver them to global markets with full quality assurance.

With deep roots in Tamil Nadu's agricultural heartland and a network spanning multiple continents, we bridge the gap between Indian farmers and international buyers — ensuring freshness, compliance, and reliability at every step.
